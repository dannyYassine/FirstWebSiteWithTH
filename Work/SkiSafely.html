SkiSafely

As for more freelance work, I was given the project template on PDF and I had figured out the internal structure of the application from the UI experience to seamlessly saving data to a server, all behind the scenes. Custom accessory views were designed and proved to be effective to give the user an easy and efficient involvement. entering their information. Ski Safely is an app for ski mountain patrollers to assist and aid skiers on their mountain. Users can input their contact information, address, emergency contact and medical history where ski patrollers are able to assist them faster and efficiently.

This information is stored on a third party server, where the mountain employees can have access to the individual’s information.

Demo Video Available @vimeo.

  • Simple and clean log in and sign up views. The user can sign up to receive newsletters and if they choose to do so, their email is registered to a list on parse.com .

  • User is first presented to an empty table view where he or she can add users to their account. Once the three first fields are filled, they can proceed or save their information. On the third view, swiping the cell can allow the user to delete an account.

  • These views represent the different input accessory views for the textfields for date, weight, and height. This allows structured data to be entered and this avoids to have no typos from the users.

  • The rest of the application is straightforward where the user could enter information for their address, emergency contact or medical history. All information is saved a on third party server on www.parse.com .
